San Francisco

San Francisco, located on the northern California coast, is renowned for its diverse culture, iconic landmarks, and rich history. Established as a key port during the California Gold Rush in 1849, it rapidly transformed into a major financial center. The city is famous for its steep hills, Victorian architecture, and attractions like the Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz Island.

With a population exceeding 800,000, it is the fourth-most populous city in California. San Francisco is also recognized for its vibrant arts scene and progressive social movements, making it a hub for innovation and cultural diversity in the United States.
